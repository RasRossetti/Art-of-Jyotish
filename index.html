    The Art of Jyotish Project

    One of the greatest blessings in my life has been the teachings of the spiritual sciences of ancient India. Both profoundly paradigm shifting and radically simple, I believe this work has the capacity to provide transformational healing for individual lives and, by extension, society as a whole. Utilizing the exciting new technology of non-fungible tokens, I am offering one-of-one editions of my oil on canvas paintings. For each digital token sold, I pledge to donate 50% of the proceeds to the Vaidyagrama Ayurvedic Healing Village. The doctors of this incredible organization are among the most knowledgeable and authentic exponents of the spiritual sciences of ancient India in the world. By promoting this ancient knowledge with cutting edge technology, I am hopeful this project will empower individuals to live their highest purpose. Vaidyagrama spreads awareness of Ayurveda, Yoga, Jyotish, Vastu and Meditation to people throughout the world, as well as uplifting the surrounding communities in rural southern India.

    About Me

    My name is Ras Rossetti and my work explores the current of divine energy which animates our material world, in order to provide the viewer with a deeper insight of where the sacred touches the profane.

    An artist of the American south, my journey has taken me all over the globe. Having been immersed in the spiritual sciences of ancient India since the age of 19, I humbly offer my consultation services to any individual in need.
